Our Mission

Reuniting Kochi, One Item at a Time

KochiRetrace was created to give the citizens of Ernakulam a transparent, fast, and secure community portal to report missing property and return lost belongings.

Why KochiRetrace?

Every day across metro stations, shopping malls like Lulu, Infopark tech campuses, and bus hubs in Vyttila or Kakkanad, citizens lose valuable items ranging from smartphones and wallets to crucial identification documents like passports and Aadhaar cards.

Before KochiRetrace, finding a lost item required fragmented searches across social media groups or physical police station visits. Our platform centralizes lost and found notices with geolocation mapping and real-time messaging, ensuring items return to their rightful owners.
