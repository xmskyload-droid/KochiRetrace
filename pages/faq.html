Everything you need to know about reporting, claiming, and returning lost property in Kochi & Ernakulam.

KochiRetrace Questions & Answers

help Is KochiRetrace free to use for citizens of Ernakulam?

Yes, KochiRetrace is 100% free for community members to report lost items, list found property, and connect with finders across Ernakulam district. We charge zero fees.

verified_user How does item ownership verification work?

When claiming a lost item, finders will ask you to verify specific details not visible in the public listing (such as IMEI numbers, purchase bills, lock patterns, unique scuffs, or matching photo ID).

location_on What areas in Ernakulam are covered by KochiRetrace?

KochiRetrace covers the entire Greater Kochi & Ernakulam area including Kakkanad, Edappally, Marine Drive, Fort Kochi, Vyttila Mobility Hub, MG Road, Aluva, Thrippunithura, and Angamaly.

shield What safety precautions should I take when meeting someone?

Always arrange exchanges in public, well-lit places during daytime—such as Kochi Metro station lobbies, police stations, or major shopping malls. Never share sensitive banking details or passwords.
