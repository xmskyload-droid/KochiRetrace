What to do if you lose your phone in Kochi

Losing your mobile phone in high-density areas like Lulu Mall Edappally, Kakkanad Infopark bus stops, or Kochi Metro requires swift action:

  1. Track via Find My Device: Use Google Find My Device (Android) or Apple Find My (iPhone) from another device immediately to trace GPS location or play a loud sound.
  2. Lodge a Report on KochiRetrace: Create a Lost Item Report on KochiRetrace specifying your last known location (e.g., Marine Drive or Vyttila Hub).
  3. Block SIM & Register on CEIR: Contact your telecom operator (Jio, Airtel, Vi, BSNL) to block your SIM card. Visit the official Government portal CEIR (Central Equipment Identity Register) to block your handset using your IMEI number.
  4. Kerala Police Pol-App Reporting: Download the Kerala Police 'Pol-App' or visit the nearest police station (e.g., Ernakulam Town South / North Police Station) to obtain a Non-Cognizable Report (NCR) for SIM replacement.
2

Lost Aadhaar Card or ID in Ernakulam: Steps to take

Identity theft prevention is vital if your original ID card is lost in Kochi:

  • Retrieve e-Aadhaar: Visit the official UIDAI portal (myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in) to download a digital e-Aadhaar PDF using your registered mobile OTP.
  • Order PVC Card: Order an official reprint PVC Aadhaar card directly from UIDAI for ₹50 delivered to your address.
  • Report on KochiRetrace: List the lost ID on KochiRetrace without revealing full sensitive identification numbers in public view.
3

Lost Passport in Kochi: Who to contact

If you are a tourist in Fort Kochi or a resident returning from abroad and lose your passport:

  1. File Police Lost Report: Immediately visit the nearest police station in Kochi (e.g., Fort Kochi Police Station or Ernakulam Central Police Station) to obtain an official FIR/Lost Report.
  2. Inform Passport Seva Kendra (PSK): Contact Regional Passport Office (RPO) Ernakulam situated at Panampilly Nagar, Kochi (Phone: 0484 2315020).
  3. Foreign Nationals: Contact your local embassy/consulate and visit the Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO) in Kochi.
4

Lost Wallet / Bank Cards in Kerala

Act immediately to prevent unauthorized transactions:

  • Block all debit & credit cards via mobile banking apps or bank customer care helplines.
  • Disable UPI apps connected to lost phone/wallet combinations.
  • Post a notice on KochiRetrace so local finders at auto stands, bus hubs, or shops can reach out.
5

Tips for Returning Found Property in Ernakulam

Good Samaritan guidelines when you discover a lost item in Kochi:

  • Verify Claimant Ownership: Ask the claiming individual to describe specific distinguishing features, unlock a phone, or produce matching photo ID.
  • Public Meeting Spot: Meet in safe, well-lit public places such as Kochi Metro Stations, Police Stations, or Lulu Mall lobby.
  • Handover to Police / Station Master: If unclaimed, deposit found items with the nearest police station or Metro Station Master.
