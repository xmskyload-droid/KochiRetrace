Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

KochiRetrace collects basic user profile information (name, email address, avatar) when you register an account, as well as listing details (item description, approximate location in Ernakulam, optional contact phone number) when you report lost or found property.

2. How Information is Used

Your information is exclusively used to facilitate the recovery and identification of lost belongings within Ernakulam district. We do not sell or share user personal data with third-party marketers.

3. Data Protection & Security

Authentication and real-time data storage are secured using industry-standard Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules.
