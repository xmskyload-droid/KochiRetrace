Terms of Service & Community Guidelines

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

1. Honest Reporting Required

Users must submit accurate information regarding lost or found property. Fraudulent claims, fake listings, or attempts to extort money for returning property will result in immediate permanent suspension and referral to Kochi City Police authorities.

2. Verification Procedures

Claimants must provide verifiable proof of ownership before receiving property. Finders are required to exercise reasonable diligence in confirming identity.

3. Moderation & Approval

KochiRetrace administrators reserve the right to review, edit, or remove any listing that violates community guidelines or public safety policies.
